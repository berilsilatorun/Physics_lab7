# Section 0: Mathematical Foundations
## 3. Proportionality (Orantı ve Kütleçekim Yasası)

**Question (Soru):**
Consider the Universal Law of Gravitation: $F = G \frac{m_1 m_2}{r^2}$.
Determine the factor by which the force $F$ changes if:
- The distance $r$ is **doubled** ($r \rightarrow 2r$).
- Both masses $m_1$ and $m_2$ are **halved** ($m_1 \rightarrow \frac{m_1}{2}$ and $m_2 \rightarrow \frac{m_2}{2}$).

---

### Solution (Çözüm)

**Step 1: Write the initial force (İlk kuvveti yazalım)**
$$F_{initial} = G \frac{m_1 m_2}{r^2}$$

**Step 2: Apply the changes to the formula (Değişimleri formüle uygulayalım)**
Substitute the new values:
- New masses: $m_1' = \frac{1}{2}m_1$, $m_2' = \frac{1}{2}m_2$
- New distance: $r' = 2r$

$$F_{new} = G \frac{(\frac{1}{2}m_1) (\frac{1}{2}m_2)}{(2r)^2}$$

**Step 3: Simplify the expression (İfadeyi sadeleştirelim)**
$$F_{new} = G \frac{\frac{1}{4} m_1 m_2}{4r^2}$$
$$F_{new} = \frac{1}{4} \cdot \frac{1}{4} \left( G \frac{m_1 m_2}{r^2} \right)$$
$$F_{new} = \frac{1}{16} F_{initial}$$

---

### Final Answer (Sonuç)
**English:** The gravitational force $F$ decreases by a factor of **16** (it becomes $\frac{1}{16}$ of the original force).
**Türkçe:** Kütleçekim kuvveti $F$, **16 kat azalır** (ilk kuvvetin $\frac{1}{16}$'sı olur).



---

### 💡 Physics Tip (Fizik İpucu)
**English:** Notice that the force is *inversely* proportional to the square of the distance. Doubling the distance alone would reduce the force to $1/4$. Adding the mass reductions further decreases it.
**Türkçe:** Kuvvetin mesafenin *karesiyle ters* orantılı olduğuna dikkat edin. Sadece mesafeyi iki katına çıkarmak kuvveti $1/4$'e düşürürdü. Kütlelerin de yarıya inmesiyle kuvvet iyice azaldı.
