## 2. Harmonic Motion (Harmonik Hareket)

**Question (Soru):** A $10 \text{ kg}$ mass is attached to a spring and oscillates according to $x(t) = 0.2 \cos(10\pi t)$ (in meters). 
1. What is the spring constant $k$? 
2. What is the total mechanical energy of the system?
(10 kg'lık bir kütle bir yaya bağlanmış ve $x(t) = 0.2 \cos(10\pi t)$ denklemine göre salınım yapmaktadır. Yay sabiti $k$ nedir? Sistemin toplam mekanik enerjisi nedir?)

---

### ### Solution (Çözüm)

**Step 1: Identify Parameters (Parametreleri Belirleyelim)**
From the equation $x(t) = A \cos(\omega t)$:
* Amplitude (Genlik): $A = 0.2 \text{ m}$
* Angular Frequency (Açısal Frekans): $\omega = 10\pi \text{ rad/s}$
* Mass (Kütle): $m = 10 \text{ kg}$

**Step 2: Calculate Spring Constant k (Yay Sabitini Hesaplayalım)**
Using the relation $\omega = \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}$:
$$k = m \cdot \omega^2$$
$$k = 10 \cdot (10\pi)^2 = 10 \cdot 100\pi^2 = 1000\pi^2$$
Using $\pi^2 \approx 9.87$:
$$k \approx 1000 \cdot 9.87 \approx 9870 \text{ N/m}$$

**Step 3: Calculate Total Mechanical Energy (Toplam Mekanik Enerji)**
The total energy $E$ in a spring system is $E = \frac{1}{2}kA^2$:
$$E = \frac{1}{2} \cdot (1000\pi^2) \cdot (0.2)^2$$
$$E = 500\pi^2 \cdot 0.04 = 20\pi^2 \text{ Joules}$$
$$E \approx 20 \cdot 9.87 \approx 197.4 \text{ J}$$

---

### ### Final Answer (Sonuç)

* **English:** The spring constant is $k = 1000\pi^2 \text{ N/m}$ and the total mechanical energy is $20\pi^2 \text{ J}$.
* **Türkçe:** Yay sabiti $k = 1000\pi^2 \text{ N/m}$ ve toplam mekanik enerji $20\pi^2 \text{ J}$'dür.
