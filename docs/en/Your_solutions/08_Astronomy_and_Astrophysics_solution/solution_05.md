## 5. Escape Velocity

**Problem:** What is the escape velocity from the Moon? Express the result in km/s and as a fraction of Earth's escape velocity ($11.2$ km/s).

### Step 1: Calculate Lunar Escape Velocity
$$v_{esc} = \sqrt{\frac{2 G M_M}{R_M}}$$
$$v_{esc} = \sqrt{\frac{2(6.674 \times 10^{-11})(7.35 \times 10^{22})}{1.737 \times 10^6}}$$
$$v_{esc} \approx 2376\text{ m/s} = 2.38\text{ km/s}$$

### Step 2: Fraction of Earth's Escape Velocity
$$\text{Fraction} = \frac{2.38}{11.2} \approx 0.21$$

**Conclusion:** The Moon's escape velocity is **$2.38\text{ km/s}$**, which is about **21%** (or roughly 1/5th) of Earth's escape velocity.
